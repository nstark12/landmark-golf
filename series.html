Series Options
Choose your ideal model, made to order with a personalized touch.
Luxury Series

One of our most popular options, the Luxury Series is the flagship of our lineup and the perfect edition to an elite course or club. The units themselves are hearty, but possess intricate aesthetic details and an easy option to add a logo display–they are literally the whole package.

Specifications

Hearty Vertical Framework (True 2” x 2”).

Solid Paneling (Rinowood: V–Groove Tonge–&–Groove, Black Forest & EasyCare: ⅝” HDPE).

Arched Accent Boards (3½” x 1½”) with Cross–Support Framework (2½ x 1½).

Hidden Pocket Holes.

Dado Slot Joinery.

Stainless Steel Hardware.

Hampton Series

The Hampton Series preceded the Luxury Series and was the first to display its thicker profile framework and arched cross–supports. Where it lacks in a readily available logo display, it makes up for in pure heartiness to stand up to the harshest of elements.

Specifications

Hearty Vertical Framework (True 2” x 2”).

Solid Paneling (Rinowood: V–Groove Tonge–&–Groove, Black Forest & EasyCare: ⅝” HDPE).

Arched Accent Boards (3½” x 1½”) with Cross–Support Framework (2½ x 1½).

Hidden Pocket Holes.

Dado Slot Joinery.

Stainless Steel Hardware.

Original Series

The classic, Original Series, where Landmark got its start. It's solid, dependable, and flawless. With the additional of V–Groove tongue–&–groove paneling on Rinowood models, the Original Series looks better than ever.

Specifications

Solid Vertical & Cross–Support Framework (1½” x 1½”).

Solid Paneling (Rinowood: V–Groove Tonge–&–Groove, Black Forest & EasyCare: ⅝” HDPE).

Hidden Pocket Holes.

Dado Slot Joinery.

Stainless Steel Hardware.

Slatted Series

The Slatted Series shows strength in number—literally. Distributing multiple slats, riveted in a circle holds a lot of weight and integrity while creating a high–end presentation on par with a Top 100 course.

Specifications

Slatted Configuration.

Solid HDPE Rings.

Rust–Free Aluminum Rivets.
