Luxury

Rinowood™ 72 Quart Potbelly Ice Chest

Luxury Series: 2¼” x 2¼” vertical framework, arched accent boards, cross–support framework, dado slot paneling and hidden pocket hole construction.

304 Hardware: Every screw, bolt, and fastener is made from 304 stainless steel, which means no rust stain drips or weakening of structural integrity from the outdoors—even from salt air or humid climates and other caustic chemicals.

Each unit includes (2) double–walled BPA–free cooler, spigot for easy draining, and compression dampers on lid.

Glides (”Feet”) Option and 36” (91.44 cm) height available for additional cost.

Important: Manually forcing lids shut is a potential safety hazard and may damage the brackets, leading to failure of the dampening mechanisms–thereby voiding our limited one year warranty policy.
